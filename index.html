06 / METHODOLOGY

Structured Design Process

Translating raw brand aspirations into strict guidelines and structural collateral. My framework guarantees premium precision.

01

Discovery & Research

I audit your current visual positioning, research key luxury competitors, and establish a creative mood board.

02

Concept & Sketching

I draft primary logotypes, map structural layouts, and test typographic pairings in pure high-contrast configurations.

03

Refinement

We review the directions, refine the favorite route, build out the brand collateral (packaging, stationary, UI frames), and tweak kerning details.

04

Delivery & Support

I bundle print-ready vectors, export packaging blueprints, compile asset guidelines, and coordinate print production vendors.

12 / INQUIRIES

Frequently Asked Questions

Timelines vary based on package scope. Consulting projects take 1–2 weeks, standard branding projects take 4–6 weeks, and full-scale corporate UI/UX and packaging architectures take 8–12 weeks. I maintain a slow, quality-first workload.
I specialize primarily in brand architecture and UI/UX styling design. For final web development (React, NextJS, Shopify systems), I collaborate directly with elite development agencies, supervising the execution to ensure design-perfect accuracy.
Branding and UI packages include 3 revision rounds. Because my discovery phase is highly rigorous, the initial creative directions are closely aligned with your brand strategy, reducing the need for extensive adjustments.
I deliver all vector source formats (.AI, .EPS, .SVG, .PDF), print-ready die-line specifications, Pantone color matching logs, interactive Figma prototype links, and a comprehensive typography guideline document.
